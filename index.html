Questions

Frequently asked

An e-visa is an electronic travel authorization issued directly by a government's online system. Unlike traditional visas, there's no need to visit an embassy or submit physical documents. You apply online, pay the government fee, and receive approval digitally — usually within days.

We guide you through the application process, review your documents, and submit on your behalf — or prepare your complete application for you to submit. We charge a small service fee on top of the official government visa fee, saving you time and reducing the risk of errors.

Processing times vary by country. Turkey and Kenya e-visas are typically issued within 24–48 hours. India's eTV can take 3–5 business days. US B1/B2 visas involve embassy appointments and take longer. We'll always give you a clear timeline upfront.

Absolutely. We use secure, encrypted systems to handle all documents. Your passport details and personal data are used solely for your visa application and are never shared with third parties.

While e-visa rejections are rare, they can happen. We'll work with you to understand why and help you reapply if possible. Our service fee policy in rejection cases will be clearly communicated before you pay.
